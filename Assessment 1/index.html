Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl, add milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Whisk until well combined.
  3. Heat butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  4. Pour in the egg mixture.
  5. Stir gently and continuously with a spatula until eggs are set but still moist.
  6. Remove from heat immediately and serve hot.
