Touhou Project

The Touhou Project is a place of fantasy set in the world of Genokyo

Key Information

Genre: Fantasy, Adventure, Shoujou

Release Date: August 30, 1998

Platform: PC, Console

Cast & Creative Team

Director: ZUN

Writer: ZUN

Characters

Hakurei Reimu: The central protagonist who embarks on a journey of keeping Gensokyo safe. She is a shrine maiden sworn to keep the balance of Gensokyo steady

Marisa Kirisame: The deuteragonist of the Touhou Project. Unlike Reimu, she is much more chaotic and specalizes in magic
